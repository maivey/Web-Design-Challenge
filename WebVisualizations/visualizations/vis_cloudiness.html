Cloudiness

Cloudiness does not seem to make much of a difference as we approach the equator. The cloudiness with respect to distance from the equator ranges from 0-100 % for all cities examined, which were between 60 degrees below and 80 degrees above the equator. Thus, a different analysis must be conducted to determine a statistically significant relationship between cloudiness and distance from the equator.
